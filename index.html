About Me

As of May, 2020, I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts from Wanger College in Theatre Performance and a Minor in Computer Science. With a small background in computers, I enrolled myself to Thinkful's program to gain the marketable skills so that I can pursue a career in both theatre and computers, taking my passion for web development to a new level!

I have always enjoyed connecting and collaborating with others. Being in the performing arts, it comes pretty naturally and is something I hope to bring into my web development career as well. Let's collaborate!
